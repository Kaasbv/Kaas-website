How it's made

The most important ingredient of cheese is of course milk. The origin and type of milk is also a great factor for the taste. This is why our milk is coming from dutch cows on the dutch grasslands. The cows get high quality food, enough space and ofcourse lots of love. They'll be outside atleast 8 hours a day and can choose when the want to be milked with our automatic milk machines. After milking it is saved in barrels.
